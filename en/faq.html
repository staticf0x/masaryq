How did this club start?

MUNI LGBT+ came to be as a club for queer folks whose goal is to survive more than one generation of students. After learning how queer students are bullied and discriminated here, we didn't have a choice but to organize and fight it collectively.

What do we expect from members?

We expect that they help organize at least one club event during the year and attend club meetings.

Why become a member?

  • Become a part of a community of inspirative people.
  • Every change we achieve will be also thanks to you.
  • Space for realizing your own ideas.
  • You will get many nontraditional and informative experiences.

Can I join, even if I don't study at MUNI?

Of course, there is no problem with that.
