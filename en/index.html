MUNI LGBT+ is a student club at Masaryk University in Brno for LGBT+ folks and allies. Our goals:

  • equalize LGBT+ folks inside MUNI and fight against discrimination;
  • create space in academy for meetings, discussion and events;
  • support coming outs and provide a feeling of solidarity;
  • offer space for meeting others and an opportunity to actively get involved in organizing events;
  • inform and start debate on queer topics.

Currently we meet on Discord, where we plan our events and host club meetings. Interested already? Don't be afraid to contact us and show interest in becoming a member!
