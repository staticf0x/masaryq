MUNI LGBT+ is a student club at Masaryk University in Brno for LGBT+ folks and allies. Our goals:

  • support the needs of LGBT+ people within MUNI, offer them an option to fight bullying and discrimination;
  • create space in academy for meetings, discussion and events;
  • support coming outs and provide a feeling of solidarity;
  • offer space for meeting others;
  • allows students to actively help organizing our activities;
  • inform and start debate on queer topics.

Currently we meet on Discord, where we plan our events and host club meetings. We also inform about our activities on Facebook and Twitter. If you'd like to get involved, have an idea or need help, just contact us.
